01 The Problem

Your company knowledge
is scattered.
Your team is guessing.

Every company has the answers buried inside its data. Most teams can't find them fast enough — or trust them when they do.

P.01

Knowledge Lives Everywhere

SOPs in Google Drive. Policies in Confluence. Customer history in tickets. No single source of truth — and no way to search across all of it reliably.

P.02

Generic AI Hallucinates

Off-the-shelf LLMs make things up. Without grounding in your actual documents, every answer is a liability risk — especially in regulated industries.

P.03

No Traceability

When an employee acts on wrong information, you can't trace where it came from. Compliance teams have no visibility into AI-generated answers.

P.04

Deployment Takes Months

Enterprise AI projects drag on. Procurement, integration, fine-tuning — by the time it's ready, requirements have already changed. You need something that ships fast.

02 The Solution

Built for the way
enterprises actually work.

Designed for teams that need reliable answers, not just impressive demos. Every feature exists to reduce risk and increase trust.

⬡

Multi-Source Connectors

Connect to documents, databases, PDFs, SharePoint, Notion, Confluence, and internal APIs. Your data stays in your systems — we reach in.

❝

Cited Answers

Every response includes source references. Teams know exactly which document, page, or section the answer came from. No more guessing.

◎

Permission-Aware Access

Retrieval respects document-level permissions. Users only see answers from content they are authorized to access — enforced at retrieval time.

≡

Audit Trails

Full logging of every query, retrieval, and answer. Compliance teams get complete visibility. Every decision is traceable to a source document.

◈

Built-In Evaluation

Measure retrieval accuracy, answer quality, and hallucination rates out of the box. Know exactly how well your knowledge assistant is performing.

◉

Fast Deployment

Ships in days, not months. Open-source core means no vendor lock-in. We build custom deployments at a fraction of enterprise software costs.

Connect Your Knowledge
